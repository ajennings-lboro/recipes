
# Guacamole
## Ingredients
* Dynamite
* Avocado
* Salt
* Lime (definitely not lemon!!)
* Coriander
* Chilli
* Pepper
* Meth
* Cocaine
* MORE Cocaine
## Method
1) Mash avocado
2) Squeeze some additional lime over the top of the guacamole to prevent turning brown.
3) Serve with a side of torilla chips, and a cold beer.
